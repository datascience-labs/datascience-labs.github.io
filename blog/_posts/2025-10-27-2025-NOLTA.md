### 소식 알림
본 연구실의 박사과정 김정수, 석사과정 박정현, 김대로, 손원석, 김아영 학생이 작성한 논문이 IEICE NOLTA 논문 트랙에 게재 승인 되었습니다.

### 세부내용
Okinawa에서 개최된 NOLTA 2025 학회에 참가하였습니다. 발표한 논문의 제목은 다음과 같습니다.
- Mitigating Hallucinations in Semantic Annotation of Administrative Documents for Digital Twin Government / [LINK](https://www.datasciencelabs.org/papers/NOLTA2025-Mitigating-Hallucinations-in-Semantic-Annotation-of-Administrative-Documents-for-Digital-Twin-Government/)
- Carbon-Aware Orchestration of Deep Learning Tasks via PaaS-Based Migration Control  / [LINK](https://www.datasciencelabs.org/papers/NOLTA2025-Carbon-Aware-Orchestration-of-Deep-Learning-Tasks-via-PaaS-Based-Migration-Control/)
- Towards Layout-Aware Estimation of the Digital Carbon Footprint in Web Environments  / [LINK](https://www.datasciencelabs.org/papers/NOLTA2025-Towards-Layout-Aware-Estimation-of-the-Digital-Carbon-Footprint-in-Web-Environments/)
- Content-guided Diffusion for Cold-start Item Recommendation  / [LINK](https://www.datasciencelabs.org/papers/NOLTA2025-Content-guided-Diffusion-for-Cold-start-Item-Recommendation/)
- Fast and Efficient Column-Level Class Linking in Table using Flash Attention / [LINK](https://www.datasciencelabs.org/papers/NOLTA2025-Fast-and-Efficient-Column-Level-Class-Linking-in-Table-using-Flash-Attention/)
